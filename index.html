About me

Most of my career was spent inside the SQL Server stack of a Guatemalan bank — designing tables, breaking down stored procedures, debugging the kind of data integrity issues that only surface after a report has already reached a VP. I care about correctness more than novelty.

These days I am pushing my stack further into Python data engineering (Pandas, Flask, pipeline orchestration) and AI quality evaluation. Every project in this portfolio uses the same techniques I applied in production — on synthetic data, fully reproducible, no proprietary systems required.

Spanish is my native language. English at B2, improving at university. Comfortable working async with teams across the Americas and Europe.
